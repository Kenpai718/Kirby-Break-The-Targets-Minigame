Kirby: Target Practice
Kirby: Break the Targets Minigame
Fan-based project developed by Kenneth Ahrens
Inspired from Smash Bros and Kirby
Debug Mute Volume
    CONTROLS:
  • (WASD) to move
  • (Space) to jump. Double tap to enable flying mode!
  • (Flying Mode) has free range of motion in the air.
       IN FM: W to ascend, S to descend, A for left, and D for right.
       Press Space again to cancel flight mode and fastfall.
  • (Move-Mouse/Left-Click) to aim and shoot
      The arrow will fly in the direction of your cursor after the shooting animation finishes.

    • OBJECTIVE:
  • Attack and break as many targets in a time limit and aim for a high score!

    • HINTS:
  • Position Kirby closer to the target to make it easier to hit!
  • Different colored targets yield different points and have different behavior.
  • Skill based bonuses are applied at the end such as accuracy and max combo!
